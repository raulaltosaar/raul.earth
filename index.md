# Raul Altosaar, artist & researcher


I build musical interfaces using custom software and hardware. I craft intense performances with these systems, sometimes in holes, on rooftops, or in the wastelands surrounding large cities. Currently, I am a master’s student at Stanford University’s [CCRMA](). Read more [about me](/about), view [my CV](), or get in touch to ask why [I believe in bugs]().
